Board Advisory

Governance Readiness Review

An independent diagnostic of your board's oversight structure — delivered as a formal report with a 90-day roadmap.

Most boards receive cyber and AI reporting. Very few have ever tested whether that reporting gives them what they actually need to govern. This review examines your governance structure, your accountability arrangements, and the quality of what reaches your board — and tells you, plainly, whether your oversight would withstand scrutiny. Delivered as a formal written report with a structured debrief.

What I Examine

This review operates entirely at the board level. I examine governance structures, not technical infrastructure.

policy

Policy and Accountability Architecture

Whether the documents that are supposed to govern cyber and AI risk actually do so — and whether accountability is specific, documented, and understood, or assumed and informal. The gap between the two is where regulatory exposure lives.

rate_review

Board Reporting Quality

Whether the reporting your board receives gives you what you need to make decisions and ask challenge questions — or whether it gives you technical assurance that creates the illusion of oversight without the substance.

account_tree

Accountability and Escalation

Whether the organisation has a clear, documented chain of accountability — who is responsible, who is accountable, who the board hears from, and under what circumstances the board is notified outside the normal cycle.

balance

Regulatory Alignment

Whether your current governance position would satisfy the expectations of ASIC, APRA, the OAIC, or the sector regulator that applies to your organisation — assessed against specific provisions, not general principles.

What You Receive

A formal Governance Readiness Report, delivered to the Chair or Risk Committee and presented in a structured 60-minute debrief session.

check_circleBoard Governance Maturity Assessment — your board's oversight rated across six domains against a five-level framework, with a clear explanation of what each rating means and what movement requires.
check_circleRACI Accountability Matrix — who is currently responsible and accountable for each risk domain, compared to what a defensible structure requires. The gaps are named.
check_circleBoard Reporting Effectiveness Assessment — your current reporting scored against four tests a regulator would apply. Includes a redesigned reporting template your board can adopt immediately.
check_circleKey Findings — two to five specific findings, each with the evidence that supports it, the regulatory provision it engages, and a concrete recommendation.
check_circle90-Day Remediation Roadmap — a prioritised action table with owners and deadlines. Designed to be tabled at your next board meeting and acted on.
check_circleDebrief Session — a structured 60-minute session with the Chair or Risk Committee to walk through the findings, confirm the roadmap owners, and answer questions.
